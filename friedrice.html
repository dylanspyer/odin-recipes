Fried Rice

Description

It's fried rice. Everyone likes it. Literally. I've never met someone who didn't like fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Teryaki (sp?)
  • Love (and oil, you also need oil)

Steps

  1. Oil the pan
  2. Add oil then precooked rice then a scrambled egg
  3. Cook it till it's done. Add vegetables if you're into that.
